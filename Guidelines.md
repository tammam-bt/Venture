# Project Submission Guidelines

Welcome to the platform! To ensure your startup idea receives the most accurate AI evaluation and is presented effectively to our investor network, please follow these submission guidelines carefully.

Our AI scoring engine evaluates your project based on Viability, Feasibility, Impact, and ROI. To do this efficiently, we require clean, structured data.

---

## 1. Required Documents & Inputs

We keep submission requirements simple and focused. Please provide the following:

### 1. Pitch Deck (PDF)

The main document. Covers everything: problem, solution, market, team, business model, and traction.

- **Format:** `.pdf` only.

### 2. Financial Projections (PDF or Excel)

Include your revenue forecast, expected costs, and burn rate. A simple version is acceptable.

- **Format:** `.pdf` or `.xlsx`.

### 3. One-Liner Form Fields (Text Inputs in App)

Please complete these text input fields directly in the app:

- Company name
- Industry / sector
- Funding amount they are seeking
- Stage (idea / MVP / revenue)

---

## 2. Formatting Best Practices for AI Evaluation

Our AI model relies on extracting context from your files. To maximize your score, optimize your documents for machine readability:

- **Avoid Complex Layouts:** Two-column text or highly stylized infographics can confuse text-extraction tools. Keep text linear where possible.
- **Use Clear Headings:** Label your sections clearly (e.g., "Target Audience", "Revenue Model"). The AI uses these headers to navigate your document.
- **Be Concise:** The model rewards clear, definitive statements over marketing fluff. Focus on data, mechanics, and concrete plans.

---

## 3. Submission Checklist

Before clicking submit, ensure:

- [ ] Your Pitch Deck is uploaded as a PDF.
- [ ] Your Financial Projections are uploaded as PDF or Excel.
- [ ] All required one-liner text fields are completed in the app.
- [ ] You have removed highly sensitive personal contact information from the documents (investors will receive this via the platform once they unlock your project).

---

## What Happens Next?

1. **AI Processing:** Upon submission, the AI engine will parse your documents and run a simulated market research analysis.
2. **Scoring:** You will be assigned a score (0-100) and receive a brief transparency report detailing the strengths and weaknesses found in your proposal.
3. **Marketplace Listing:** Your project (Title, Short Summary, and Score) will instantly appear on the investor dashboard.
4. **Matchmaking:** If an investor unlocks your project, you will be notified to arrange a direct meeting.
